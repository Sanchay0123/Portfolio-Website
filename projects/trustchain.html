TRUSTCHAIN

A decentralized, tamper-proof blockchain system tracking medicines across the entire supply chain to eliminate fraud and ensure authenticity.

View Website code

The Counterfeit Crisis.

Global healthcare supply chains are fragmented and rely on centralized, easily manipulated databases. Counterfeit medicines slip through the cracks, endangering patients and costing the industry billions. The challenge was creating a trustless environment where the physical journey of a product is irrefutably bound to a digital ledger.

Decentralized Logistics.

Asset Tokenization

Manufacturers register physical medicines on the blockchain by minting unique digital identifiers (e.g., MED-001) paired with precise production batch metadata.

account_tree

Smart Contract Routing

State-change functions require multi-signature approval. As a shipment moves from distributor to pharmacy, the asset's digital ownership is transferred securely on-chain.

verified_user

Trustless Verification

Patients act as the final node. By scanning the medicine ID, they query the blockchain directly to view the unbroken, cryptographic proof of the drug's journey.
